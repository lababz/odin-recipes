Ingredients

For 4 people, you'll need:

    • 240g red lentils
      1 fresh cauliflower
      25cl coconut milk
      4 tsp concentrated tomato (paste)
      1 yellow onion
      4 tsp curry powder
      280 rice

    • Steps

    • Cook the rice (about 10 minutes in a pot of salted boiling water). Drain and keep warm.
      In the meantime, peel and cut the onion into small pieces.
      Cut the cauliflower into small florets and wash them.
      Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a pan. Add the onion, tomato paste and curry (add a little cumin if you wish). Saut√© over medium heat, stirring for 2 minutes.
      Pour the red lentils, add the cauliflower and pour 20cl of water per portion. Add salt and pepper.
      Add the coconut milk. Mix and cook covered over medium heat for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
      Serve with the rice, add a few parsley leaves if you wish and re-season according to your tastes, it's ready! Bon appetit!
