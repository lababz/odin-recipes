Ingredients

For 1 burger, you'll need:

    • 1 burger bun
      1 beef chopped steak
      1/2 tsp mayonnaise
      1 tsp tomato ketchup
      30g dried tomatoes
      20g ewe cheese (tome)
      Fresh parsley leaves

    • Steps

    Ready, set, go!

    • Cut the cheese into thin slices.
      Toast the inside of your burger buns in a very hot pan (15 to 30 seconds is enough). Optional: butter them.
      In a pan, add the steak and cook for 2 minutes on one side
      Turn them over, then add the cheese on top. Cook covered for 2 more minutes.
      Meanwhile, make the sauce by mixing the mayonnaise and the ketchup.
      Optional: if you have it at home, add half a teaspoon (per person) of whiskey or cognac to the sauce.
      Place the steak on the bun and top it with the sauce. Add the tomatoes and parsley. Close the burger, serve, it's ready!

    • Bon appetit!
